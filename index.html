Your Ayurvedic Constitution

Vata Dosha

Your assessment shows a dominance of Vata dosha, which is composed of the elements Air and Space. People with Vata constitution tend to be creative, energetic, and lively when in balance.

Physical Traits
Thin build, dry skin, cold hands and feet
Mental Traits
Quick mind, creative, adaptable
When Imbalanced
Anxiety, insomnia, digestive issues

Tips for Balancing Your Dosha

  • Follow a regular daily routine with consistent meal times
  • Favor warm, moist, and grounding foods
  • Practice calming activities like gentle yoga and meditation
  • Keep warm and avoid cold, windy environments
  • Get adequate rest and avoid overexertion
